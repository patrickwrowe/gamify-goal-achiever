About

Gamify is a web application that helps you achieve your goals by breaking them into smaller, manageable tasks, inspired by quests in games. This approach keeps you motivated by showing progress and providing a sense of accomplishment with each completed task. By focusing on one step at a time, large goals become achievable without feeling overwhelming.

Canned Promps to Avoid Making Many Requests to the API
