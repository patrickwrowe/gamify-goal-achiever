About

Some text about me

Gamify is a web application that helps you achieve your goals by breaking them down into smaller tasks. This is done by using the logic from quests in games.

By breaking down your goals into smaller tasks, you can see your progress and stay motivated to achieve your goals. Each small task you complete gives you a sense of accomplishment and brings you one step closer to your ultimate goal. This approach not only makes large goals more manageable but also helps you maintain a steady pace and avoid feeling overwhelmed.
